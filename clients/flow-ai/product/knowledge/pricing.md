# Flow AI — Pricing

> Agent uses this file to answer pricing questions. Do not quote prices outside these tiers.

---

## Pricing Model

Flow AI is priced as a monthly subscription per client, with a one-time onboarding fee.

| Tier | Monthly | Onboarding | Included |
|------|---------|------------|---------|
| Starter | SGD 500/mo | SGD 1,500 | 1 WhatsApp number, FAQ + lead capture, up to 500 conversations/month |
| Growth | SGD 900/mo | SGD 2,000 | 1 WhatsApp number, full booking flow + CRM, up to 2,000 conversations/month |
| Scale | Custom | Custom | Multiple numbers, custom integrations, priority support |

**Note:** "Conversation" = one complete customer interaction thread (start to end), not per-message.

---

## What's Included in All Tiers

- Unlimited agent updates (change your pricing, hours, services — no redeployment fee)
- Human escalation flow (alert to your team's WhatsApp)
- CRM logging (Supabase — every conversation, every booking)
- 24/7 uptime monitoring
- Monthly performance report

---

## What's Not Included

- Meta/WhatsApp Business API fees (billed directly by Meta — approximately USD 0.005–0.025 per conversation depending on category)
- Google Calendar API costs (minimal — included in standard Google Workspace)
- Custom third-party integrations (e.g., Salesforce, HubSpot) — quoted separately

---

## Payment Terms

- Monthly subscription billed on the 1st of each month
- Onboarding fee due on contract signing
- Payment via bank transfer (DBS)
- 30-day notice required to cancel subscription

---

## Agent Guidance

If a prospect asks for a custom quote or says the tiers don't fit:
> "Our pricing is tailored for each business — let me connect you with our founder to walk through a fit assessment and put together the right package for you."

Do not negotiate pricing. Escalate pricing negotiations to the founder.
