# Flow AI — Target Verticals

> Use this file to assess lead fit during qualification. High-fit verticals should be escalated to founder. Low-fit should be captured for nurture.

---

## Primary Target Verticals (Phase 1)

### 1. HVAC & Home Services
- **Examples:** Aircon servicing, plumbing, electrical, pest control, cleaning
- **Why they fit:** High inbound WhatsApp volume (booking requests, quotes), repetitive FAQs, appointment-heavy workflow, field teams who can't manage chat during jobs
- **Key pain points:** Manual booking via WhatsApp, missed messages, no-shows, no CRM
- **Reference client:** HeyAircon (Singapore)

### 2. Aesthetics & Wellness Clinics
- **Examples:** Aesthetic clinics, beauty salons, physiotherapy, TCM, dental
- **Why they fit:** Appointment-driven, WhatsApp is primary booking channel, high inquiry volume pre-appointment, privacy-sensitive (escalation to human for medical questions)
- **Key pain points:** Front desk overwhelmed with WhatsApp, no-show rates, manual appointment reminders
- **Booking complexity:** Medium (appointment slots, service types, practitioner availability)

### 3. Real Estate Agencies
- **Examples:** Property agents, leasing teams, co-working space operators
- **Why they fit:** Constant inbound from property listings, WhatsApp is standard channel for viewings, need lead capture + qualification before agent time is spent
- **Key pain points:** Unqualified leads wasting agent time, slow response = lost deal, no systematic follow-up
- **Booking complexity:** Low (viewing slot booking, no complex service variations)

### 4. Insurance Brokerages
- **Examples:** General insurance brokers, financial advisers, motor insurance
- **Why they fit:** FAQ-heavy (coverage questions), lead qualification critical before adviser engagement, WhatsApp used for client servicing
- **Key pain points:** Repetitive coverage questions, slow first response, no lead tracking
- **Booking complexity:** Low (consultation booking only)

## Secondary / Emerging Verticals

| Vertical | Fit Signal | Notes |
|----------|------------|-------|
| F&B (delivery/catering) | High order volume via WhatsApp | Custom order flow needed — Phase 2 |
| Education (tutors, enrichment) | Trial booking + FAQ heavy | Good fit; lower urgency |
| Logistics (last-mile) | High volume but complex | Custom integration risk |
| E-commerce | Low fit | WhatsApp checkout not our core use case |

---

## Lead Scoring Guide

| Signal | Score |
|--------|-------|
| In a primary target vertical | +2 |
| 50–200 WhatsApp messages/week | +2 |
| 200+ WhatsApp messages/week | +3 |
| Has a dedicated person managing WhatsApp today | +1 |
| Mentions missed messages or slow responses as pain | +2 |
| Team size 3–20 people | +1 |
| Already using WhatsApp Business (not personal) | +1 |

**Escalate to founder if total score ≥ 5.** Capture for nurture if score < 5.
