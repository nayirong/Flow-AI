## What Is Live Today

### 1. WhatsApp AI Assistant
Your AI assistant is active on your WhatsApp Business number. Customers message in, and the assistant responds within seconds — 24/7, no manual effort needed from your team for routine enquiries and bookings.

### 2. Service Enquiry Handling
The assistant can answer questions about all your services — general servicing, chemical wash, chemical overhaul, gas top up, and aircon repair — including pricing by unit count and BTU size. If a customer asks about something outside the assistant's knowledge, it escalates to you rather than guessing.

### 3. End-to-End Booking Flow
When a customer wants to book a job, the assistant handles the full flow automatically:
- Collects all required details: name, address, postal code, service type, number of units, preferred date, and AM (9am–1pm) or PM (1pm–6pm) time window
- Checks your Google Calendar in real time for slot availability
- Creates a Google Calendar event instantly if the slot is free
- Sends the customer a booking confirmation via WhatsApp

Bookings made through the assistant appear in your calendar exactly like manually created events.

### 4. Human Escalation — Automatic Alerts
When a situation needs your attention, the assistant escalates immediately and alerts you via WhatsApp. This happens automatically in three cases:
- **Slot conflict:** The customer's requested slot is already taken — the assistant informs the customer and notifies you to arrange an alternative
- **Reschedule or cancellation request:** The assistant shares your rescheduling/cancellation policy and hands the conversation to you — no automated changes are ever made to existing bookings
- **Out-of-scope query:** If a customer asks something the assistant cannot reliably answer, it escalates rather than guessing

Once escalated, the AI assistant pauses for that customer. It sends a single holding message ("Your request has been passed to our team — they'll be in touch shortly") and waits. When you reply to the alert to indicate you've handled it, the assistant automatically resumes for that customer.

### 5. Customer and Booking Records
Every customer interaction and booking is automatically recorded in your database. You can view:
- All bookings with status, date, time window, service type, and customer details
- Full customer profiles with interaction history and booking count
- Complete conversation log per customer

Your data also syncs to Google Sheets so you can view and share booking records in a familiar format.

### 6. Pricing and Content — Self-Managed
Your services, pricing, and policies are stored in a database that the assistant reads in real time. Any updates you request take effect on the very next customer message — no technical changes or restarts needed.
