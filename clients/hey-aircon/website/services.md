# Hey Aircon — Services

Professional aircon services for homes and businesses across Singapore.

---

## 1. Aircon General Servicing

Our friendly experts follow a proven 10-step process for every service. Enjoy flexible options with affordable one-time servicing or value-packed yearly plans.

**What's included:**
- Cleaning of air filters, front panel, and fan coil
- Checking of drainage and water tray
- Inspection of refrigerant level and electrical components
- Testing of overall unit performance

---

## 2. Aircon Chemical Wash

Our chemical cleaning removes deep-set dirt and residue, restoring your aircon fan to deliver cool, refreshing air efficiently.

**Best for:**
- Units that haven't been serviced for a long time
- Aircons with persistent odours or weak airflow
- Units with mould or bacteria buildup inside

---

## 3. Aircon Chemical Overhaul

An aircon overhaul involves fully dismantling all internal components, thoroughly cleaning them with chemical solutions, and rinsing them with water to restore optimal performance.

**Best for:**
- Severely dirty or neglected units
- Aircons leaking water persistently
- Units with very poor cooling performance despite regular servicing

---

## 4. Gas Top Up

Our technicians carefully check and top up your aircon's refrigerant, so you can enjoy cool, comfortable air again.

**Refrigerant types supported:**
- R32
- R410A

**Signs you may need a gas top-up:**
- Aircon blows warm or slightly cool air
- Ice forming on the pipes
- Hissing or bubbling sounds from the unit

---

## 5. Aircon Repair

We identify any aircon issues and ensure that all components of your unit are functioning properly.

**Common repairs:**
- Water leaking from indoor unit
- Aircon not turning on
- Strange noises or vibrations
- Remote control or thermostat issues
- PCB board and compressor faults
