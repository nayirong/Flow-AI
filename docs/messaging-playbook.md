## 3. Brand Voice & Tone

### Core Principles

**Direct, not clever.** Lead with the answer. No unnecessary preamble. Say what you mean.

**Credible, not salesy.** Sound like a smart founder who's built something real. Not a marketing agency pushing hype.

**Honest about limitations.** HeyAircon launched April 2026 — no 6-month results yet. That's okay. Use first-principles ROI math instead of fake testimonials.

**Conversational authority.** Write like you're explaining this to a peer over coffee. Use contractions. Short sentences. No jargon unless the audience uses it.

**Founder voice, not corporate.** You're selling to business owners. They can smell corporate marketing a mile away. Sound like a real person.

---

### Words to Use

- WhatsApp (always specific, never generic "chat")
- Automate, handle, filter, qualify, escalate
- Structured data, business intelligence, institutional knowledge
- Human-in-loop, handoff, escalation
- Time saved, admin hours freed up, revenue recovered
- Live in 4 weeks, fast time-to-value
- Singapore, Malaysia, SEA (be geographically specific)

---

### Words to Avoid

Never use:
- Game-changer, revolutionary, disruptive
- Leverage, synergy, paradigm
- Transform, empower, unlock
- Cutting-edge, next-generation
- Seamlessly (overused)
- Exponential growth

Minimize:
- AI-powered (obvious — just describe what it does)
- Solution (vague — say "agent" or "system")
- Platform (overused in pitch mode — fine in docs)

---

### Sentence Structure

**Good:**
- "Your receptionist spends 15 hours a week answering the same questions. Flow AI handles that automatically."
- "The agent checks your Google Calendar in real time and books the appointment if the slot is free."
- "You only pay more when the system is actively generating bookings for you."

**Bad:**
- "Our revolutionary AI-powered platform seamlessly leverages cutting-edge technology to transform your customer engagement paradigm."
- "We empower service businesses to unlock exponential growth through intelligent automation."
- "Flow AI is a next-generation solution that disrupts the traditional customer service model."

## 5. Discovery Call Script

**Goal:** Qualify the prospect, understand their pain, and determine if Flow AI is a fit — before pitching.

**Duration:** 30 minutes (discovery: 15 min, demo: 10 min, close: 5 min)

---

### Opening (30 Seconds)

"Hey `[FIRST_NAME]`, thanks for making time. I know you're busy, so I'll keep this tight.

Here's how I'd like to use the next 30 minutes: I'll ask you a few questions about how you're currently handling customer inquiries on WhatsApp — what's working, what's painful, how much time your team spends on it. Then I'll show you a quick demo of how Flow AI handles that automatically. And if it makes sense, we can talk about what a build for `[BUSINESS_NAME]` would look like.

Sound good?"

---

### Discovery Questions (5–7 Questions)

Ask in a conversational flow. Don't interrogate. Listen for pain signals.

#### 1. Inquiry Volume
"How many customer inquiries does `[BUSINESS_NAME]` get per week across WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, website, etc.? Roughly."

*Listen for:* 50+ per week = strong fit. <20 per week = marginal fit (ROI harder to justify).

---

#### 2. Current Handling Process
"Walk me through what happens when a customer messages you on WhatsApp right now. Who replies? How fast? What do they typically ask?"

*Listen for:* Manual replies, receptionist/admin handling, repetitive questions, slow response time, after-hours inquiries ignored.

---

#### 3. Repetition Rate
"What percentage of those inquiries are the same questions over and over? Like pricing, availability, service area, that kind of thing?"

*Listen for:* 50%+ repetition = strong fit for automation.

---

#### 4. After-Hours Pain
"What happens when someone messages you at 9pm or on Sunday asking if you have slots available? Does anyone reply, or does it wait until Monday morning?"

*Listen for:* After-hours inquiries lost or delayed = clear ROI signal.

---

#### 5. Current CRM / Data Capture
"Do you currently log those WhatsApp conversations anywhere? Like a CRM or spreadsheet? Or does it just stay in the chat?"

*Listen for:* No structured data = Flow AI's intelligence layer is a major win. Already using a CRM = integration opportunity.

---

#### 6. Tech Comfort Level
"Are you comfortable with tools like Google Calendar, Google Sheets, basic online software? Or do you prefer to keep things simple and offline?"

*Listen for:* Tech-comfortable = faster onboarding. Tech-averse = more hand-holding needed, but still doable.

---

#### 7. Decision Timeline
"If this makes sense, what's your timeline for making a decision? Are you looking to solve this in the next month, or is this more exploratory right now?"

*Listen for:* Active pain + fast timeline = hot lead. Exploratory = nurture, not close today.

---

### Demo Transition (30 Seconds)

"Got it. Sounds like you're getting `[X]` inquiries per week, and `[Y]%` of those are repetitive stuff your team is answering manually. That's exactly what Flow AI handles automatically.

Let me show you what this looks like in practice. I'll share my screen and walk you through a real WhatsApp conversation with the AI agent we built for HeyAircon. Just watch how it handles a customer inquiry from start to finish."
